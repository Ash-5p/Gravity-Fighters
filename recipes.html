Recipes

A good workout plan is nothing without the right nutrition to ensure your body is getting everything it needs to grow, recover and perform in the gym.

Here are a few simple recipes to help keep your meal plans interesting without spending hours in the kitchen.

Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Overnight Oats

    Ingredients

  • 40g Oats
  • 50g Chocolate Protein Powder
  • 15g Cocoa Powder
  • 30g Peanut Butter
  • 150ml Skimmed Milk (Or Milk Substitute)

    Preparation

  1. Measure out the oats, protein powder, and cocoa powder into a bowl and mix together.
  2. Pour milk into bowl with dry ingredients
  3. Add peanut butter and mix thoroughly until a batter-like consistency is achived.
  4. Refridgerate overnight.
  5. Enjoy!
